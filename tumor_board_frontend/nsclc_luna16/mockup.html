Why the modifiers? The disease-context echo says risk_bucket=HIGH, max_diameter_mm=26.84, driving_feature=max_diameter_mm. Under the NCCN LUNG-1/LUNG-2 nodule-branch (≥8 mm) rules, PET/CT staging and tissue diagnosis are the two gating diagnostic actions before any therapy choice — so biopsy and PET/CT get positive audit weights. Tumor board falls a rank because it's a coordination step, not a diagnostic gate.
